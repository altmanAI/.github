# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

AltmanAI is built on the belief that AI should serve human dignity — the same principle applies to how we treat each other here. We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community for everyone who contributes to, uses, or engages with AltmanAI projects.

## Expected behavior

- Treat others with respect, patience, and good faith.
- Engage in technical disagreement professionally — critique ideas, not people.
- Welcome differing perspectives, backgrounds, and levels of experience.
- Assume good intent, and communicate clearly when it's missing.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Abusive, demeaning, or hateful language
- Doxxing or sharing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, report it to **security@altmanai.tech** with the subject line `[CoC]`. Reports will be handled discreetly and taken seriously.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this standard. Violations may result in a warning, temporary restrictions on issue or PR activity, or a permanent ban from AltmanAI repositories and community spaces, at maintainers' discretion.

## Governance

Final judgment on Code of Conduct enforcement decisions rests with AltmanAI's founder-led governance model, as described in `GOVERNANCE.md`.
