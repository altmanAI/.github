## What AltmanAI is building

AltmanAI develops practical AI systems that strengthen human judgment, planning, creativity, accountability, and execution. We do not treat human oversight as a disclaimer added after development. Human authority, reviewability, security, and evidence are designed into the operating model.

Our public engineering standard is:

> **Human authorization → AI execution → Proof-of-Impact validation**

Every material initiative should make five things inspectable:

1. **Who authorized the objective**
2. **What the human and AI systems each contributed**
3. **What evidence supports the result**
4. **What risks, limitations, and unknowns remain**
5. **Who remains accountable for the decision or release**

## Operating principles

| Principle | Engineering meaning |
|---|---|
| **Human authority** | People define objectives, approve consequential decisions, and retain the ability to stop, challenge, or reverse automated work. |
| **Proof over promises** | Claims should be supported by code, tests, artifacts, sources, demonstrations, or traceable decision records. |
| **Transparent assistance** | Material AI assistance is disclosed where it shapes public code, documentation, research, or decisions. |
| **Accurate capability** | Proposed, experimental, beta, production, deprecated, and archived work are labeled honestly. |
| **Secure by design** | Secrets, personal data, permissions, dependencies, supply-chain risk, and failure modes are treated as core product concerns. |
| **Human impact** | Systems are evaluated by whether they improve human capability without hiding responsibility or eroding meaningful control. |

## Advanced-AI and superintelligence readiness

AltmanAI does **not** claim to have created superintelligence. We are building governance and engineering foundations intended to remain useful as AI systems become more capable, autonomous, multimodal, and deeply integrated into real-world workflows.

Our readiness model emphasizes:

- explicit authorization boundaries and revocable permissions;
- least-privilege access for agents, tools, credentials, and data;
- traceable actions, provenance, logs, and versioned decision records;
- human confirmation for consequential or irreversible operations;
- staged deployment, evaluation gates, rollback paths, and incident response;
- resistance to prompt injection, data exfiltration, deceptive behavior, and unsafe tool use;
- honest uncertainty, escalation, and refusal when evidence is insufficient;
- continuous review of societal, security, privacy, accessibility, and dependency risks.

See the organization-wide **[AI Systems Policy](https://github.com/altmanAI/.github/blob/main/AI_SYSTEMS_POLICY.md)** and **[Governance Standard](https://github.com/altmanAI/.github/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md)**.

## The P.A.I.H.I. standard

Material work is reviewed through five lenses:

- **Proof** — What evidence supports the claim, change, or outcome?
- **Alignment** — Does the work match the authorized objective and mission?
- **Integrity** — Are limitations, uncertainty, assistance, and conflicts represented honestly?
- **Humanity** — Does the system preserve dignity, privacy, agency, accessibility, safety, and meaningful review?
- **Impact** — What useful result occurred, and how can it be inspected or measured?

P.A.I.H.I. is not a substitute for domain-specific legal, security, safety, medical, financial, or regulatory review. It is an organization-wide accountability layer.

## Contributing

We welcome focused contributions from developers, researchers, designers, security practitioners, technical writers, operators, and other builders who believe advanced AI should increase human capability while keeping responsibility visible.

Before substantial work:

1. Review the target repository’s README, license, security policy, and contribution rules.
2. Open a focused issue that defines the problem, evidence, expected value, risks, and realistic scope.
3. Disclose material AI assistance.
4. Include tests, validation, screenshots, sources, or reproducible review steps.
5. Keep confidential information, credentials, personal data, and private vulnerability details out of public channels.

Start with **[CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/altmanAI/.github/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)**.
