# AltmanAI — Project Governance

## Structure

- **BDFL (Benevolent Dictator for Life):** Blake Hunter Altman — Founder & Chief Executive Officer, AltmanAI by Altman Family Group LLC.
- **Official AI Technology Partner:** AltmanAI Model 2.0 — for advisory, drafting, research, development support, documentation architecture, governance preparation, and human-first AI system refinement.
- **Source of truth:** Blake Hunter Altman is the final decision-maker over all official AltmanAI records, documentation, product direction, and public statements.
- **Downstream repositories:** All product and infrastructure repositories in the AltmanAI ecosystem mirror approved artifacts, documents, workflows, and product logic defined at the organization level in this repository.

## Decision process

1. Proposals — governance changes, new documentation, product direction questions — are documented as GitHub issues in the relevant repository.
2. Proposals are reviewed by repository maintainers and, where applicable, discussed with the Official AI Technology Partner for drafting and research support.
3. Decisions are finalized only through an official approval entry from Blake Hunter Altman.
4. The explicit approval phrase for any governance-level or public-facing change is:

   **"All Clear for Impact"**

No governance change, public profile update, or contributor-standard revision is considered final until this phrase is given.

## Principles

- Precision
- Transparency
- Human authorization
- Ethical execution
- Proof-of-Impact validation
- Practical AI usefulness
- Trustworthy software development
- Human-centered product design
- Responsible research and deployment

## Scope

This governance model applies to the `.github` organization repository and, by default, to all AltmanAI product and infrastructure repositories unless a specific repository defines its own governance file.
