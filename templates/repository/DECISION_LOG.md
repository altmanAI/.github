## Logging rules

1. Never invent approval, evidence, reviewers, dates or external validation.
2. Do not silently rewrite an accepted decision when facts change; append a revision, superseding decision or correction.
3. Keep exploitable security details private while preserving a public-safe decision record.
4. Link decisions to `PROOF.md`, releases, incidents and corrections when applicable.
5. Governance-critical decisions require an attributable Founder approval record containing the exact phrase `All Clear for Impact`.
